Talks and Presentations

Dec 3-5, 2013

Vector-valued Time Series Analysis

Herzberg Institute of Astrophysics (Dec 3), University of Victoria (Dec 4), and University of British Columbia (Dec 5): Colloquium tour through beautiful British Columbia discussing the representation of time-series lightcurves, feature selection, and classification.

Nov 13-15, 2013

State-based Models for Lightcurve Classification

Hotwiring the Transient Universe: 3: Invited talk regarding parameterization and classification of lightcurves having vector-valued (e.g. multi-passband) properties.

Sept 19-20, 2013

Image Differencing for LSST

LSST Core Data Processing Software Design Review: Presentation to an internal review committee of the image subtraction algorithms for the proposed LSST project, identified as a national scientific priority in reports by several National Academy of Sciences and federal agency advisory committees. This review is in preparation for the Final Design Review of the project, in anticipation of construction funding beginning in FY 2014.

Aug 12-16, 2013

Data Analysis and Image Subtraction in the Era of LSST

First La Serena School for Data Science: Two invited presentations on data analysis given in La Serena, Chile to the next generation of American and Chilean astronomers.

July 25, 2013

Searching for Habitable Exoplanets with the Kepler Spacecraft

Pacific Science Center: Interactive presentation to the Pac-Sci Discovery Corps outreach Program
